Muzhe Wu 吴沐哲

Human-Computer Interaction Researcher

I am a first-year PhD student at the University of Michigan (CSE) advised by I create assistive and learning technologies to enhance people's abilities in their everyday interactions with the physical world.

Previously, I earned a master's degree from Carnegie Mellon University (HCII), where had the pleasure of working with Dr. David Lindlbauer on Extended Reality and Dr. Hong Shen on Responsible AI Tools. I earned dual bachelor's degrees from the University of Michigan and Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
