About Me

I am a first-year master student at the Human-Computer Interaction Institute (HCII) at Carnegie Mellon University, majoring in Educational Technology and Applied Learning Sciences.

My research interest lies in Human-AI Interaction, where I am specifically keen on understanding how humans can learn from AI and how AI can learn from humans. Towards this goal, I have explored various domains in my undergrad, including intelligent tutors, multimodal learning and generalist agent, advised by Prof. Anhong Guo, Prof. Xu Wang, Dr. Jim Fan, and Dr. Veronica Perez Rosas.
